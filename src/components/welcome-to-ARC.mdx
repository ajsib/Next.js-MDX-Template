## [Welcome to PROJECT-ARC](./docs/)

PROJECT-ARC is a modular, scalable framework for building, showcasing, and deploying applications through a configuration-first, component-driven architecture.

---

### The Atrium
Developer lab and main app ecosystem.  
Where all ongoing projects are built before shipping.  
Stack: Next.js, Express, PostgreSQL.

---

### The Nucleus
Developer application containing the component library, algorithm library, and complete data/documentation flow to power work in The Atrium.  
Organized into atoms, molecules, and features.

---

### The Obelisk
Infrastructure and deployment automation.  
CLI to start new projects from templates.  
Handles containerization of all services and prepares them for deployment.
